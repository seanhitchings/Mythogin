Hero Journeys across Culture

Examine epic stories with fresh perspective—each deep dive organized by Critique, Fix, and Impact.

What You’ll Find Here

Not every story intended to be epic, actually functions as such. Many stories that try to be epic fall short— and when they do, they tend to receive notoriety. But those are worth examining to see what was missing. The good news is that there are some you've probably not seen before.

Each of the following stories is examined with a critical eye—to see what was done right, what was done wrong, and what influence it has upon the wider culture.

Within each topic, you’ll find essays structured around three questions:

  • Critique — What the story attempts, and where it fails or succeeds.
  • Fix — How the story could be strengthened to reach its potential.
  • Impact — What the story communicates to audiences and culture at large.
